Note:: Sent to the Shackleton Expanse to act as a bridge builder to new peoples and civilizations. She carefully chose her crew for this purpose.

Astrid Alder built a reputation in Starfleet as a First Contact specialist. She is a remarkable ability to keep contact open with new civilization and build bridges between them and the Federation.

Now, Captain Alder is assigned to the USS Hudson to form new alliances far away on the far side of the Klingon and Romulan empires, in the Shackleton Expanse. For this mission, she has hand picked members of her crew to handle a wide variety of scientific, military, and diplomatic situations.
